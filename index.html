Trivolivia Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Trivolivia (“Trivolivia,” “we,” or “the App”) is a trivia and learning application designed to help users explore topics through AI-generated quizzes. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. ⸻ Information You Provide When you use Trivolivia, you may voluntarily provide: • Topic text or prompts (for example, a subject you want to learn about) • Optional feedback or support inquiries you send to us ⸻ Information Collected Automatically Trivolivia does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or advertising identifiers. The App may store limited, non-personal data locally on your device, including: • App settings and preferences • Gameplay progress (such as completed quizzes or difficulty level) • Cached trivia questions to improve performance and reduce repetition This data remains on your device unless you choose to delete the App. ⸻ How Information Is Used Information is used solely to: • Generate trivia questions and answers based on your selected topics • Improve app reliability and performance • Respond to support requests if you contact us Trivolivia does not use your information for advertising or profiling. ⸻ Third-Party Services Trivolivia uses third-party services to provide core functionality: • Google Gemini API Topic text and related prompt context are sent securely to Google’s Gemini API to generate trivia content. Google processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policies and terms. Trivolivia does not share personal data with third parties for marketing or tracking purposes. ⸻ Data Storage and Retention • Locally stored app data remains on your device. • Trivolivia does not maintain user accounts. • We do not sell, rent, or trade user information. • You can remove locally stored data at any time by deleting the App. ⸻ Tracking Trivolivia does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. The App is not used for targeted advertising. ⸻ Children’s Privacy Trivolivia is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address the issue. ⸻ Your Choices • You may delete the App to remove any data stored locally on your device. • You may contact us with privacy-related questions or concerns. ⸻ Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
